The Color TV Game Is The First Colored Console Ever Known To Mankind. Made In 1977. Definitely A Collectable And Could Be Sold For A $125 To $150 Dollar Range. This Console Runs 6 Games. Newer Models Have A More Variety Of Games Which Can Be Found Online As Well. These 4-Bit Games Were Some Of The Littiest Games To Have During The Era Of Gaming



The NES Is An Family Enterntainment System That Ran 8-bit Games Made In 1983. This Had A Variety Of Games Like Mario, Metroid, And The Legend Of Zelda. This Is A Collectible That Can Be Sold At A Price Range $90 To $150, Depending On What Bundle You Buy



The SNES Is An Family Enterntainment System That Is A Upgraded Version Of The NES. This Console Was Made In 1990. The SNES Is More Advanced Than The NES With Better Graphics Better Gameplay And Easier Controls. The SNES Has A Price Range Of $80 To $190, Depending On Where You Buy It From



The Virtual Boy Is The First "Virtual Reality" Game Ever Made, Released In 1995. Considering That The Console Was Discontinued Due To Consumers Getting Nausea And Headaches While Using The System You Can't Get Your Hands On One Unless You Know Someone Selling The System



Nintendo 64 Is The Console That Started 3D Gaming. This Console Was Released In 1996. This Console Is Better Than Previous Consoles Because Of Its Graphics. The Price Range Of The N64 Is $40 To $110, Depending Were You Buy It



The Gamecube Is The Console That Started Game Franchise's Like Super Smash Bros. This Was Released In 2001. This Console Is Family Based With Many Games Released With Co-op. The Price Range For The Gamecube Is $30 To $100



The Wii Is The Console Specifically For Family Gaming As Well. Including Features Like Mii Characters. Also Included Games Like Wii Sports. The Graphics Were Very Advanced At This Time. The Price Range For The Wii Is $250 To $500



The Wii U Is The Console That Started The Handhelds And Console Combination. The Wii U Has Amazing Graphics With A Good Amount Of Memory To Play Many Games. The Wii U Also Started The Era Of Amiibo, Figurines That Can Be Used In Game. This Console Was Released In 2012. The Price Range For The Wii U Is $199 To $250. This Also Introduced Online Play For Nintendo.



The Nintendo Switch Is The Console That Continued The Handhelds + Console Trend. The Switch Has Great Graphics. And Is More Portable Than The Wii U Was. This Released In 2017 With A Price Of $200 To $550.
